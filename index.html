— Our Story —

Built in Glasgow,
by vapers, for vapers.

Our wee shop started in 2017 as a single counter on Albion Street, run by two ex-smokers who were sick of being sold the wrong kit by folk who'd never used one. The idea was simple: honest advice, proper testing, and liquids we'd actually vape ourselves.

Today we mix every house flavour in the back of the shop, stock the gear we trust, and take the time to set your kit up before you leave. No pressure, no patter — just the right setup for you.
